TITLE : THE LOST LETTER 
 characters : -Sara:A curious 12 year old girl who loves solving mysteries. -Grandpa john : an old man who owns a book store. -Lily:Friend of sara who accompanies her in adventures.
setting: -A small town where everyone knows each other and are very friendly. - Grandpa's bookstore,filled with old books and hidden secrets.-A mysterious mailbox at the end of the road
plot: One day while exploring grandpa's book store sara and lily found an unopened and old letter hidden inside a book.The book doesn't has any sender or receiver name and it was 50 years old letter,so sara and lily decided to find the mystery behind the letter
